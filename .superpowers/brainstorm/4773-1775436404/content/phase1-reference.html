Design Notes
Keep
Background grid + steel rails
Perfect Arkanoid atmosphere. Keep exactly as-is for XenoGotchi Phase 1.
Keep
3 eggs at top (orange / cyan / green)
Maps directly to Crimson / Obsidian / Ivory egg types. Colors are right. Shape is right.
Keep
Shared yellow brick grid
The "Thermal Seal" shared block mass. All players must break through this first before hitting egg-specific blocks.
Adapt
6 capsules → 3 per-egg block columns
The capsule pillars are the right visual concept but there are 6 here (Arkanoid power-ups). For XenoGotchi we need exactly 3 — one column per egg, color-matched, with different block counts per egg type.
Adapt
Orange paddle → biomechanical/alien design
The paddle could become a bio-scanner probe platform. Same shape, but reskinned to fit the Alien aesthetic — dark metallic with Weyland-Yutani markings?
Open Question
What are the 3 egg colors in-game?
Orange/Cyan/Green here — but your spec says Crimson (red), Obsidian (black/dark), Ivory (cream). Do we swap the in-game colors to match the spec names, or keep this warmer palette?
Open Question
Per-egg column block counts
Obsidian = most blocks (dense, hardest)? Crimson = medium? Ivory = fewest (easier to crack, tank build)? Or all equal?
Open Question
"Direct hit to hatch" mechanic
Once all your egg's blocks are cleared, the egg becomes hittable directly. One clean hit = hatches. Keep this two-step win? Or auto-hatch on last block?
